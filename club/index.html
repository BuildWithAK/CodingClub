About Us

Coding Club is a vibrant community of passionate learners, creators, and problem-solvers. Our mission is to empower students with practical coding skills, foster collaboration, and inspire innovation through hands-on activities, lectures, and challenges.

  • Mission: To make coding accessible and fun for everyone.
  • Activities: Weekly lectures, coding challenges, project showcases, and peer mentoring.
  • Resources: Curated notes, daily tasks, and regular tests to track progress.
  • Community: Connect with fellow students, share ideas, and grow together.
  • Contact: Email us at info@codingclub.com
